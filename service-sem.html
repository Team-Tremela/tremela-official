Search Engine Marketing Services

Search engine marketing (SEM), commonly referred to as pay-per-click (PPC), encompasses a myriad of paid advertisement channels. Typically, this involves bidding for specific keywords, both branded and non-branded, with the intention of delivering the most conversions for a given budget.

However, that power becomes greatly compromised when PPC campaigns are executed poorly–delivering high expenditure but negligible results.
